About Us

Mindscapes offer a unique set of structured thinking tools that help boost the ability to generate impactful, insightful, innovative and creative ideas in advertising. These tools are built using common thinking patterns, drawn from award winning ideas from around the world.

The app contains the full set of Mindscapes’ unique, creative, thinking tools for advertising. Each tool guides and assists advertising professionals in the creation of new ideas and campaigns, enabling you to exploit your inborn creative talent more effectively, thereby elevating and enriching your creative output.

Each tool in this app contains:
  • A brief explanation about the tool
  • Step by step guidelines for applying the tool
  • Examples of selected case studies exemplifying the tool and the pattern of thinking it represents
  • Tips and suggestions for using the tool

For any technical support, feedback or further questions about a Mindscapes Creativity Training Program please contact us at info@themindscapes.com
